About Us

Welcome to Annadanam, where we believe in making a positive impact by addressing food insecurity in our community. Our mission is to reduce hunger and minimize food waste by connecting surplus food with those in need.

Founded in 2023, our organization has been dedicated to creating a hunger-free community through the generosity of donors, the hard work of volunteers, and strategic partnerships with local businesses and organizations.

Join us in our journey to create a world where no one goes to bed hungry. Together, we can make a difference.

where we believe in the power of sharing and giving. Our mission is to provide nutritious meals to those in need, fostering a sense of community and compassion.


Our Story

Annadanam was born out of a passion for helping others and a realization that we could make a difference in the lives of those facing food insecurity. Since our humble beginnings, we have grown into a community-driven force for good.


Get Involved

We invite you to join us in our mission to combat hunger. Whether you want to volunteer your time, make a donation, or simply spread the word about our cause, every action counts.
